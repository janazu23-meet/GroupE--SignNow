Sign Now is an international start-up that specializes in on-demand sign language translation for the deaf community - anywhere and anytime..

Our company is made up of a combination of both deaf and non-deaf employees. The professional aspect is managed entirely by our deaf managers, who stand at the forefront of all our activity. Over the past 5 years, we have succeeded in bringing international recognition to sign language.

Goal 3 - Good Health and Well Being

Stabilize the deaf sign language speaking community, which constitues 1.25% of the world's population, as integral part of the global community entitled to recieve real-time accessible services for effective, accessible, and independant conduct.

Goal 11 - Sustainable Community

Positioning and assisting the deaf community to become a stable and proud community, capable of standing on its own two feet and contributing more to the general public.
